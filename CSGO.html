Counter Strike: Global Offensive

Counter Strike: Global Offensive or CS:GO is a first-person multiplayer shooter by Valve. It has many game modes but the most popular is the 5 vs 5 competitive mode where T-side must try to plant a bomb at one of the available bomb sites and the CT-side must try to defend the sites and defuse the bomb if it was planted by the T-side.

CS:GO has been played in eSports for years and big tournaments are hosted every year with world-star-level players and teams taking part to compete for prizes up to millions of euros.
