Destiny 2

Destiny 2 is an MMORPG from Bungie. The game has multiple open-world maps which are located on different planets in the solar system. The player chooses their character, or "Guardian" from three classes and multiple elemental subclasses with unique abilities which they then use to fight the Darkness and its allies, such as the Roman army inspired Cabal, who use brute force and massive arsenal of weapons in their conquest of the universe.

The Guardians’ powers and abilities were first granted by The Traveler, an entity of Light that has terraformed many planets in the Solar system and has given humanity technologies and capabilities far beyond our imagination, and that is what the Guardians are protecting.

Destiny 2 is a multiplayer game and many activities, especially the higher-end ones require a fireteam of up to six players, but most of the entry level activities are playable solo. The game is "Free to play", but most of the playable content and play areas are DLC or Season pass -locked.
